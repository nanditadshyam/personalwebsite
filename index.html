Who am I?

I am a dedicated and ambitious analyst that is always looking to spearhead new initiatives. I graduated from Rutgers Business School - New Brunswick with a Bachelor's of Science in Business Analytics & Information Technology with a minor in Spanish.
I am passionate about unlocking the potential of big data and enterprise solutions to drive data-driven decision-making, and utilizing BI tools to transform intricate datasets into meaningful insights. I am committed to honing my skills in data analytics methodologies and staying at the forefront of emerging trends to contribute meaningfully to the evolving landscape.
I consider myself a global citizen having lived in many different countries and having professional proficiency in Spanish. I'm enthusiastic about furthering my knowledge and experience in technology-integrated finance. I hope to use my passion for cultures and languages in my work and make a global impact.
